We describe the syzygy spaces for the Segre embedding \mathbb{P}(U)\times\mathbb{P}(V)\subset\mathbb{P}(U\otimes V) in terms of representations of {\rm GL}(U)\times {\rm GL}(V) and construct the minimal resolutions of the sheaves \mathscr{O}_{\mathbb{P}(U)\times\mathbb{P}(V)}(a,b) in D(\mathbb{P}(U\otimes V)) for a\geqslant-\dim(U) and b\geqslant-\dim(V). Also we prove some property of multiplication on syzygy spaces of the Segre embedding.
