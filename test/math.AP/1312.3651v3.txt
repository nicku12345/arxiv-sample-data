Earlier versions of these lecture notes have been used at the Cork Summerschool on Theory and Mathematics Modelling of Ultrashort Pulse Propagation (2013), and as a part of a graduate course in the theory of nonlinear waves in the period 2014-2016. This version has an improved typography and numerous typoes has been corrected. It also has been extended with a section where the method of multiple scales is applied to boundary layer problems for ODEs. The document consists of 78 pages and contains 9 figures.
