This technical report accompanies the following three papers. It contains the computations necessary to verify some of the results claimed in those papers.   [1] Carolyn Chun, Deborah Chun, Dillon Mayhew, and Stefan H. M. van Zwam. Fan-extensions in fragile matroids. In preparation. [2] Carolyn Chun, Dillon Mayhew, Geoff Whittle, and Stefan H. M. van Zwam. The structure of binary Fano-fragile matroids. In preparation. [3] Ben Clark, Dillon Mayhew, Geoff Whittle, and Stefan H. M. van Zwam. The structure of {U2,5, U3,5}-fragile matroids. In preparation.
