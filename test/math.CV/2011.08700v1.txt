Let f be function that is analytic in the unit disk {\mathbb D}=\{z:|z|<1\}, normalized such that f(0)=f'(0)-1=0, i.e., of type f(z)=z+\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} a_n z^n. If additionally, \[ \left| \left(\frac{z}{f(z)}\right)^2 f'(z) -1\right|<\lambda \quad\quad (z\in{\mathbb D}), \] then f belongs to the class {\mathcal U}(\lambda), 0<\lambda\le1. In this paper we prove sharp upper bound of the modulus of the fifth coefficient of f from {\mathcal U}(\lambda) in the case when 0.400436\ldots \le\lambda\le1.
