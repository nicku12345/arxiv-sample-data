We generalize the main result of math.RA/9608214 concerning the convex embeddings of a chain Gamma in a lexicographic power Delta^Gamma. For a fixed nonempty chain Delta, we derive necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of nonempty solutions Gamma to each of the lexicographic functional equations (Delta^Gamma)^{<=0} simeq Gamma, (Delta^Gamma) simeq Gamma, and (Delta^Gamma)^{<0} simeq Gamma.
