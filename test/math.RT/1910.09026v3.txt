This paper concentrates on the Sakellaridis-Venkatesh Conjecture for \mathrm{U}(2)\backslash \mathrm{SO}_5. We give an explicit Plancherel formula for \mathrm{L}^2(\mathrm{U}(2)\backslash \mathrm{SO}_5) and \mathrm{L}^2((N,\psi)\times T\backslash \mathrm{PGL}_2\times \mathrm{PGL}_2). Then we determine the dimension of \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{U}(2)}(\sigma,\mathbb{C}) and give a proof of relative character identity. Finally, we show a decomposition of globally \mathrm{U}(2)-period.
