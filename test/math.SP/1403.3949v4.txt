We prove a Weyl asymptotics N(r) = c r^d + {\mathcal O}_{\epsilon}(r^{d - \kappa + \epsilon}), \forall\, 0< \epsilon \ll 1, for the counting function N(r) = \sharp\{\lambda_j \in {\mathbb C} \setminus \{0\}:\: |\lambda_j| \leq r^2\}, r>1, of the interior transmission eigenvalues (ITE), \lambda_j. Here 0<\kappa\leq 1 is such that there are no (ITE) in the region \{\lambda\in {\mathbb C}:\: |{\rm Im}\:\lambda|\geq C(| {\rm Re}\:\lambda|+1)^{1-\frac{\kappa}{2}}\} for some C>0.
