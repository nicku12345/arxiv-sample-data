The necessary and sufficient conditions are found for the two sequences \left\{\mu_n \right\}_{n=0}^{\infty} and \left\{a_n \right\}_{n=0}^{\infty} to be the spectrum and the norming constants respectively, for a boundary value problem -y'' + q \left( x \right) y = \mu y, y \left(0\right)=0, y\left( \pi \right)\cos \beta + y'\left( \pi \right)\sin \beta = 0, \beta \in \left( 0, \pi \right), with q \in L_{\mathbb{R}}^1 \left[0, \pi \right].
