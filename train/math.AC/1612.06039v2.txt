Let \mathbb{F}_{q} be a finite field of characteristic 2 and O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+} be the 2-dimensional orthogonal group of plus type over \mathbb{F}_{q}. Consider the standard representation V of O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+} and the ring of vector invariants \mathbb{F}_{q}[mV]^{O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+}} for any m\in \mathbb{N}^{+}. We prove a first main theorem for (O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+},V), i.e., we find a minimal generating set for \mathbb{F}_{q}[mV]^{O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+}}. As a consequence, we derive the Noether number \beta_{mV}(O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+})=\max\{q-1,m\}. We construct a free basis for \mathbb{F}_{q}[2V]^{O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+}} over a suitably chosen homogeneous system of parameters. We also obtain a generating set of the Hilbert ideal for \mathbb{F}_{q}[mV]^{O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+}} which shows that the Hilbert ideal can be generated by invariants of degree \leqslant q-1=\frac{|O_2(\mathbb{F}_{q})^{+}|}{2}, confirming Derksen-Kemper's conjecture \cite[Conjecture 3.8.6 (b)]{DK2002} in this particular case.
