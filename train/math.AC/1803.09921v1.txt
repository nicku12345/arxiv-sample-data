Primary hyperideals have been introduced and studied in multiplicative hyperrings. In this paper, we intend to study extensively primary hyperideals of multiplicative hyperrings with absorbing zero and prove some results regarding them. Also, we describe Cu- ideals of multiplicative hyperrings which are particular classes of hyperideals. In the last section, we introduce 2-absorbing primary hyperideals and investigate the properties of this notion in commutative multiplicative hyperrings.
