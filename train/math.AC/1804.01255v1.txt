Let (R,\mathfrak{m}) be a Noetherian local ring of dimension d>0 with infinite residue field. Let M be a finitely generated proper R-submodule of a free R-module F with \ell (F/M) < \infty and having rank r. In this article, we study the fiber multiplicity f_0(M) of the module M. We prove that if (R,\mathfrak{m}) is a two dimensional Cohen-Macaulay local ring, then f_0(M)\le br_1(M)-br_0(M)+\ell (F/M)+\mu(M)-r, where br_i(M) denotes the i^{th} Buchsbaum-Rim coefficient of M.
