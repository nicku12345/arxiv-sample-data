This note (which makes no claim to novelty) presents a proof of the separable rational connectedness of smooth cubic hypersurfaces, in any characteristic, by showing how to explicitly construct very free curves (of degree 3) on them.   -----   Cette note (qui ne pr\'etend pas \`a l'originalit\'e) d\'emontre la s\'eparable rationnelle connexit\'e des hypersurfaces cubiques lisses, en toute caract\'eristique, en construisant explicitement des courbes tr\`es libres (de degr\'e 3) trac\'ees dessus.
