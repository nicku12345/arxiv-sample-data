Let    S_{m,n}(q):=\sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1-q^{2k}}{1-q^2}   (\frac{1-q^k}{1-q})^{m-1}q^{\frac{m+1}{2}(n-k)}.    Generalizing the formulas of Warnaar and Schlosser, we prove that there exist polynomials P_{m,k}(q)\in\mathbb{Z}[q] such that    S_{2m+1,n}(q) =\sum_{k=0}^{m}(-1)^kP_{m,k}(q)   \frac{(1-q^n)^{m+1-k}(1-q^{n+1})^{m+1-k}q^{kn}}   {(1-q^2)(1-q)^{2m-3k}\prod_{i=0}^{k}(1-q^{m+1-i})},  and solve a problem raised by Schlosser. We also show that there is a similar formula for the following q-analogue of alternating sums of powers:      T_{m,n}(q):=\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{n-k}   (\frac{1-q^k}{1-q})^{m}q^{\frac{m}{2}(n-k)}.
