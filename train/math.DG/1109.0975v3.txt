Let \mathcal{J}^1 be the real form of a complex simple Jordan algebra such that the automorphism group is \mathrm{F}_{4(-20)}. By using some orbit types of \mathrm{F}_{4(-20)} on \mathcal{J}^1, for \mathrm{F}_{4(-20)}, explicitly, we give the Iwasawa decomposition, the Oshima--Sekiguchi's K_{\epsilon}-Iwasawa decomposition, the Matsuki decomposition, and the Bruhat and Gauss decompositions.
