In this article we show that if {\cal V} is the variety of polynilpotent groups of class row (c_1,c_2,...,c_s),\ {\mathcal N}_{c_1,c_2,...,c_s}, and G\cong{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_1}}\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_2}}\stackrel{n}{*}...\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf{Z}}_{p^{\alpha_t} } is the nth nilpotent product of some cyclic p-groups, where c_1\geq n, \alpha_1 \geq \alpha_2 \geq...\geq \alpha_t  and  (q,p)=1 for all primes q less than or equal to n, then |{\mathcal N}_{c_1,c_2,...,c_s}M(G)|=p^{d_m} if and only if G\cong{\bf {Z}}_{p}\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf {Z}}_{p}\stackrel{n}{*}...\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf{Z}}_{p } (m-copies), where m=\sum _{i=1}^t \alpha_i and d_m=\chi_{c_s+1}(...(\chi_{c_2+1}(\sum_{j=1}^n \chi_{c_1+j}(m)))...). Also, we extend the result to the multiple nilpotent product G\cong{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_1}}\stackrel{n_1}{*}{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_2}}\stackrel{n_2}{*}...\stackrel{n_{t-1}}{*}{\bf{Z}}_{p^{\alpha_t} }, where c_1\geq n_1\geq...\geq n_{t-1}. Finally a similar result is given for the c-nilpotent multiplier of G\cong{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_1}}\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf {Z}}_{p^{\alpha_2}}\stackrel{n}{*}...\stackrel{n}{*}{\bf{Z}}_{p^{\alpha_t}} with the different conditions n \geq c and  (q,p)=1 for all primes q less than or equal to n+c.
