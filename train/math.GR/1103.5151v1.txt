We present an explicit structure for the Baer invariant of a free nth nilpotent group (the nth nilpotent product of infinite cyclic groups, \textbf{Z}\st{n}* \textbf{Z}\st{n}*...\st{n}*\textbf{Z}) with respect to the variety {\cal V} with the set of words V=\{[\ga_{c_1+1},\ga_{c_2+1}]\}, for all c_1\geq c_2 and 2c_2-c_1>2n-2. Also, an explicit formula for the polynilpotent multiplier of a free nth nilpotent group is given for any class row (c_1,c_2,...,c_t), where c_1\geq n.
