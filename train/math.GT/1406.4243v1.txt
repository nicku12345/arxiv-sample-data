We further sharpen higher type adjunction inequalities of P. Ozsv\'ath and Z. Szab\'o on a 4-manifold M with a nonzero Seiberg-Witten invariant for a Spin^c structure \frak{s}, when an embedded surface \Sigma\subset M satisfies [\Sigma]\cdot [\Sigma]\geq 0 and |\langle [\Sigma],c_1(\frak{s})\rangle|+[\Sigma]\cdot [\Sigma]\geq 2b_1(M).
