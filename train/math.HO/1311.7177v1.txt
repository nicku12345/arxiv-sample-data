A collection of selected papers of Gino Fano, containing most of his research papers in Italian, is now available on line at the site of Biblioteca Digitale Italiana di Matematica. The address is: http://www.bdim.eu/ The realization of this collection on line is part of a joint project of Unione Matematica Italiana (UMI) and Societa' Italiana per la Matematica Applicata e Industriale (SIMAI). The present submission to arXiv is the introduction to the collection and an announcement of it as well.
