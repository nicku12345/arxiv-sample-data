First year mathematics students are often initially overwhelmed with the transition from A Level mathematics to university level mathematics, especially when being acquainted with the new language of pure mathematics. This report outlines and evaluates a project which introduced online quizzes as a form of assessment to not only academically support students in their transition by providing an extrinsic motivation to engage them with their learning material but also to emphasize the small details within proofs, such as defining notation, which are not necessarily emphasized by written homework assignments.
