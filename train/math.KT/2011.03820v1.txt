For an infinite field F, we study the cokernel of the map of homology groups H_{n+1}(\mathrm{GL}_{n-1}(F),\mathbb{k}) \to H_{n+1}(\mathrm{GL}_{n}(F),\mathbb{k}), where \mathbb{k} is a field such that (n-2)!\in \mathbb{k}^\times, and the kernel of the natural map H_{n}\big(\mathrm{GL}_{n-1}(F),\mathbb{Z}\big[\frac{1}{(n-2)!} \big] \big) \to H_{n}\big(\mathrm{GL}_{n}(F),\mathbb{Z}\big[\frac{1}{(n-2)!} \big]\big). We give conjectural estimates of these cokernel and kernel and prove our conjectures for n\leq 4.
