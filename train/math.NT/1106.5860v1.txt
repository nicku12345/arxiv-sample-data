In this paper we construct a pseudorandom multisequence (x_{n_1,...,n_r}) based on kth-order linear recurrences modulo p, such that the discrepancy of the s-dimensional multisequence (x_{n_1+i_1,...,n_r+i_r})_{1 \leq i_j \leq s_j, 1 \leq j \leq r} 1 \leq n_j \leq N_j, 1 \leq j \leq r is equal to O((N_1 ... N_r)^{-1/2} \ln^{s+3r}(N_1 ... N_r)), where s=s_1 ... s_r, for all N_1,...,N_r with 1 < N_1 ... N_r \leq p^k
