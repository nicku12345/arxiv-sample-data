Given an affine Kac-Moody Lie algebra \tilde{\mathfrak{g}}[\sigma] of arbitrary type, we determine certain minimal sets of annihilating fields of standard \tilde{\mathfrak{g}}[\sigma]-modules. We then use these sets in order to obtain a characterization of standard \tilde{\mathfrak{g}}[\sigma]-modules in terms of irreducible loop \tilde{\mathfrak{g}}[\sigma]-modules, which proves to be a useful tool for combinatorial constructions of bases for standard \tilde{\mathfrak{g}}[\sigma]-modules.
