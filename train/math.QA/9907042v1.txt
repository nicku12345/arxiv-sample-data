We introduce the tangent space  T({\rm H}_q)  on the quantum hyperboloid ( {\cal A}_{0,q}^c ) and equip it with an action on  {\cal A}_{0,q}^c  being a deformation of the action of vectors fields on functions. An embedding  \displaystyle sl(2)_q \longrightarrow T({\rm H}_q)  of q-deformed Lie algebra  sl(2)  being an analogue of the anchor  \displaystyle sl(2) \longrightarrow \Vect({\rm H})  is called "quantum anchor".
