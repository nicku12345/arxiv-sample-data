We give a characterization of \tau-rigid modules over Auslander algebras in terms of projective dimension of modules. Moreover, we show that for an Auslander algebra \Lambda admitting finite number of non-isomorphic basic tilting \Lambda-modules and tilting \Lambda^{op}-modules, if all indecomposable \tau-rigid \Lambda-modules of projective dimension 2 are of grade 2, then \Lambda is \tau-tilting finite.
