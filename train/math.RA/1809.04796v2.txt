In this paper, we study extensions between two finite irreducible conformal modules over the Schr\"odinger-Virasoro conformal algebra and the extended Schr\"odinger-Virasoro conformal algebra. Also, we classify all finite nontrivial irreducible conformal modules over the extended Schr\"odinger-Virasoro conformal algebra. As a byproduct, we obtain a classification of extensions of Heisenberg-Virasoro conformal modules.
