We construct and classify the free U(\mathbb{C}L_0\oplus \mathbb{C}M_0\oplus\mathbb{C}Y_0)-modules of rank 1 over the Schr\"{o}dinger-Virasoro algebra \mathfrak{sv}(s) for s=0.Moreover, we show that the class of free U(\mathbb{C}L_0\oplus\mathbb{C}M_0)-modules of rank 1 over the Schr\"{o}dinger-Virasoro algebra \mathfrak{sv}(s) for s=\frac{1}{2} is nonexistent.
