The absolute continuity of the spectrum for the periodic Dirac operator  \hat D=\sum_{j=1}^n(-i\frac {\partial}{\partial x_j}-A_j)\hat \alpha_j + \hat V^{(0)}+\hat V^{(1)}, x\in R^n, n\geq 3,  is proved given that either A\in C(R^n;R^n)\cap H^q_{loc}(R^n;R^n), 2q > n-2, or the Fourier series of the vector potential A:R^n\to R^n is absolutely convergent. Here, \hat V^{(s)}=(\hat V^{(s)})^* are continuous matrix functions and \hat V^{(s)}\hat \alpha_j=(-1}^s\hat \alpha_j\hat V^{(s)} for all anticommuting Hermitian matrices \hat \alpha_j, \hat \alpha_j^2=\hat I, s=0,1.
