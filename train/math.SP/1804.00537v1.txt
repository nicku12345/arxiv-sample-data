We estimate from below the bottom of the combinatorial spectrum for \operatorname{PSL}(2,\mathbb{Z}) associated to the system of generators \left\{\left(\begin{smallmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{smallmatrix}\right),\left(\begin{smallmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{smallmatrix}\right)\right\}. By combinatorial spectrum, we refer to the spectrum of the combinatorial Laplace operator on the corresponding Cayley graph.
